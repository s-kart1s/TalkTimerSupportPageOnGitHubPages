Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

TalkTimer ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information:

  • Timer settings: Timer names, durations, and Pomodoro configurations created by the user
  • App preferences: Volume settings, notification settings, and other user preferences

All of this information is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to external servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or videos
  • Device IDs or IP addresses (though Google Ads may collect these as described in Section 6)
  • Usage statistics or access logs

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the timer functionality
  • Saving and restoring user preferences
  • Improving app features

4. Data Storage

All data is stored as local storage on your device. Nothing is sent to cloud services or external servers.

5. Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not directly provide, disclose, or share your personal information with third parties. However, we use Google Ads to display advertisements, and Google may collect and process certain information (such as cookies, device identifiers, and IP addresses) to serve personalized ads. For details on how Google collects and uses this information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Advertising & Analytics

Google Ads

The App uses Google Ads to display advertisements. To serve personalized ads based on your interests, Google Ads may collect and process the following information:

  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • Device identifiers
  • IP addresses
  • Information about your interaction with ads and content

You can control Google's ad personalization settings through your Google Account settings.

For more information on how Google collects and uses data for advertising, please see Google's Advertising Technology Policy.

Analytics

The App does not use Google Analytics or similar usage-tracking services beyond what Google Ads requires.

7. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you when a timer ends or counts down. These notifications are generated on-device and do not require communication with external servers.

8. Voice Playback

Voice announcements for remaining time are played using audio files built into the App. No internet connection or access to external servers is required.

9. Data Deletion

When you uninstall the App, all data stored on your device is automatically deleted. You can also delete individual timers and settings from within the App at any time.

10. Security

The App uses your device's standard security features to protect your data. However, the security of the device itself (screen lock, encryption, etc.) is your responsibility.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time without prior notice, in response to changes in laws or additions to app features. Any updated policy takes effect as soon as it is posted on this page.

13. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Support page.
